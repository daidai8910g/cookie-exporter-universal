# Privacy Policy for Cookie Exporter Universal

**Last updated: July 9, 2026**

## Overview

Cookie Exporter Universal is a browser extension that allows users to view and export cookies from their current browser session. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**This extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data with third parties.** Specifically:

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not collect browsing history
- We do not collect cookie data beyond your local browser session
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not communicate with any external servers

## Data Processing

All data processing occurs entirely within your local browser:

- Cookie data is read from your browser's cookie store using the Chrome Cookies API
- Cookie data is displayed in the extension popup for your review
- When you choose to export, data is formatted locally and either copied to your clipboard or downloaded as a file directly to your computer
- No data is ever sent to any server

## Permissions

This extension requests the following permissions:

- **cookies**: Required to read cookie data from your browser for display and export
- **activeTab**: Required to detect the current tab's domain for automatic cookie filtering
- **host_permissions (\<all_urls\>)**: Required to read cookies from any domain you choose to query

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and no other purpose.

## Third Parties

This extension does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services.

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
